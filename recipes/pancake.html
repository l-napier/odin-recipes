Peculiar Pancakes

Pancake Pump. Instructions Below

  1. Mix flour, milk & eggs in a bowl
  2. Either follow another recipe for precise instructions, or guess
  3. If it is too thicc, add more milk
  4. If it is too wet, add more flour
  5. Once mixed to desired thickness, melt butter on pan then pour pancake batter on hot pan
  6. Flip when it looks ready
  7. Serve to plate when cooked.
  8. Eat with desired toppings.
