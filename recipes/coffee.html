Cold Coffee

Coffee that hopefully won't curdle. Instructions below.

  1. Fill kettle with water. Electric not stove top (ew)
  2. Turn kettle on
  3. Prepare glass with instant coffee granules as per packet instructions
  4. Once kettle is hot, pour hot water into glass with coffee granules
  5. Stir glass with a spoon
  6. Add in milk - dairy or alternative
  7. Add sugar if desired
  8. Stir again with spoon
  9. If drink has curdled, dispose of safely and repeat from step 1 with a different milk
  10. Enjoy!
